Quizbot


Introduction


The Quizbot is a basic companion robot designed to help students study for a test by responding to answers in an electronic quiz. The Quizbot may be programmed to nod when an answer is correct, shake its head when an answer is incorrect, and do a dance when the quiz is completed!
